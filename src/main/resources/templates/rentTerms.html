Rent terms

We offer to rent a car on the most favorable terms. Car rental in our company is carried out for a period of at least 1 day. The countdown starts from the moment of signing the vehicle acceptance certificate. For economy class cars, a minimum driving experience is sufficient, for cars costing over $ 70 (at the rate of the National Bank of Belarus), driving experience is required at least 2 years All cars are insured under the CASCO policy, in case of an accident, you must immediately notify us and call the traffic police.
Car rental rates include:
  • car rental cost
  • insurance against theft, traffic accidents and damage by third parties
Car rental rates do not include:
  • the cost of fuel and windscreen washer
  • payment for parking
  • payment of fines for traffic violations
  • car wash and cleaning
  • punctures and damage to tires and rims
  • damage to the interior of the car
Rent payment terms:
Car rental is paid in full in cash upon signing the contract.
Rental deposit
  • for renting an economy and middle class car (worth up to $ 70), a deposit of $ 50 (at the rate of the National Bank of Belarus)
  • for business class cars, experience from 5 years and age not less than 25 years
  • p.s. for citizens of the Republic of Belarus with a driving experience of up to 2 years, the minimum deposit is $ 100 (at the rate of the National Bank of Belarus)
  • for renting a premium class car, a deposit from $ 100 to $ 300 (at the rate of the National Bank of Belarus)
  • for renting a car traveling outside the Republic of Belarus from $ 200 (at the rate of NB.RB.)
  • for non-residents of the Republic of Belarus, the deposit ranges from $ 100 to $ 300 (at the rate of NB.RB.), depending on the chosen car and your driving experience.
